Never Remember Your Credentials. After you login once.

Set the Remember Me switch on the Login screen and CloudERD will save your credentials on your iPad. Next time when you want to login, just tap the blue arrow against the Username and you can choose from all previously saved credentials.

Search For Objects. By API name or Label name.

CloudERD lets you search Objects both by API Name and Label name. After you have found the Object, tap object once to add to the Diagram and tap again to remove from the Diagram.

Standard And Custom Objects. Colored differently.

Almost all the Apps use both Standard and Custom Objects and you can view them together in your Diagram. CloudERD colors them differently so that it's easy for you to understand the Diagram.

Master-Detail And LookUp. Related but differently.

CloudERD shows the relationships between Objects differently for Master-Detail and LookUp. For Master-Detail relationship a plain line is shown with arrow pointing to the Master and for LookUp relationship a dotted line is shown with arrow pointing to the referred Object.

Related Objcts. Add them with ease.

To add related Objects, both for Master-Details and LookUps just tap and hold on any Object in the Diagram and select from pop-up menu. A Remove option is also provided in the pop-up menu if you want to remove the Object from the Diagram.

Fields Of An Object. Just double tap.

CloudERD lets you see the Fields of an Object with their Data Type and also shows you which Fields are mandatory. Just double tap on any Object in the Diagram and you can see the whole Schema of an Object.

Share It Everywhere. Mail, print and social.

CloudERD lets you share your Diagrams with others by Email, Twitter, Facebook and Print or save to Camera Roll too.
